Wioletta Swierkot is a strategic transformation leader with over 13 years of cross-sector experience guiding organisations through complex change. Her career spans healthcare, NHS estates, supply chain, government contracting, and the social impact sector — environments where structured governance, financial accountability, and operational precision are critical to success.

She specialises in bridging the gap between executive ambition and disciplined programme delivery. By integrating rigorous evaluation frameworks such as Cost-Benefit Analysis (CBA) and Social Return on Investment (SROI), Wioletta ensures that strategic initiatives are evidence-based, financially justified, and capable of delivering measurable long-term value.

As an MSP® Foundation Certified programme professional, she applies internationally recognised governance standards to align portfolios with core business objectives, mitigate risk, and strengthen accountability across transformation programmes. Her approach combines strategic clarity with practical execution — ensuring that change is not only implemented, but sustained.

Alongside her professional practice, Wioletta is completing a Doctorate in Business Administration (DBA), focusing on Evidence-Based Decision Making and programme leadership within complex organisational environments. Her research informs her consultancy, bringing academic rigour and analytical depth to executive-level decision processes.

Through Think & Grow BMS, she partners with organisations ready to modernise systems, optimise performance, and demonstrate measurable financial and social impact with confidence.
